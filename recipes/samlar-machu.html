Samlar Machu

Types of Samlar Machu

Samlar machu has many types!

  1. Samlar machu yuon (សម្លរម្ជូរយួន) with lobster, fish or chicken, pineapple, dried fish and tomatoes;
  2. Samlar machu srae (សម្លម្ជូរស្រែ) with fresh and dried fish, crab and green papaya;
  3. Samlar machu kroeung (សម្លរម្ជូរគ្រឿង) with beef or pork, yellow kroeung, Ipomoea aquatica, eggplants and dried jamalac.
  4. Samlar machu ktis (សម្លរម្ជូរខ្ទិះ​) with pork, pineapple and coconut milk.
